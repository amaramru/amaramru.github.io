Malcha Mahal, New Delhi

9/nov/2004

Built by Firuz Shah Tughlaq, Malcha Mahal is located in the Raisina Hills of Chanakyapuri, New Delhi. Next to it is the Delhi Earth Station of Indian Space Research Organization. The property was attained by the Indian Government that was seized by the British. Begum Wilayat protested for her land and finally got hold of her property after confining herself with her 2 children and a dog in the VIP lounge. Soon after, the Mahal turned into more like a ghostly resident making it one of the haunted places in Delhi. Lizards, snakes, and all types of insects made the Mahal their home. The walls started turning to black. Out of distress, Begum committed suicide by swallowing the crushed diamonds. The children, Prince Riaz and Princess Sakina, lived with the dead body for 10 days. Now, this is something eerie. Many journalists went to take the interview of the children and a few didn’t come back. It has been claimed that Princess Sakina has already died as no one has seen of her. They had 27 dogs to guard them, and now only 9 are left. As of the recent news, the prince too had died ending the legacy of Oudh clan. .

Twins Village, Kerala

The Kodinhi village is home to 2000 families, and of course, this is not the mystery we are talking about. The village is known to have a large number of twins. Since 1949, when the first twins were born, the village is seeing a large number of twins birth. As of today, there are 200 twins recorded in the village. Many doctors and scientists have done a lot of researches, but the mystery remains the same.

Mass Bird Suicide, Assam

In Assam, there is a village Jatinga located in Dima Hasao. From past many years, an unfortunate event takes place in the months of September to October. Many migratory birds come here and crash themselves to death. No one has ever been able to know the reasons, not even scientists. Many attempts have been taken to stop this but of no use.

Magnetic Hills, Ladakh

Ladakh is a place of sheer beauty, but there is one mystery that has fascinated people to the core. The magnetic hill of Ladakh is a place where one can defy gravitational power. If you will leave your bike or car with the brakes locked in the hill, it will start rolling itself. Many theories like an optical illusion and other have been given to support the mystery

Homes without doors and windows, Maharashtra

Shani Shingnapur is a small village in Maharashtra and is a home to a fascinating mystery. Not a single house in the entire village has doors or windows. Not only is this but there no locker in any house where one can keep their precious and expensive items. No one dares to steal the belongings because it has been said that the village is blessed by Lord Shani and the one stealing the items will be punished. This is the village with zero crime rates. If someone has tried to steal anything, it gets back to the owner the very next day.

There are plenty of mysterious places in India, but these places top the list.
